Gingerbeer (5L-6%)

## ingredients
- 1kg ginger
- active ginger bug
- 5L water
- 1kg sugar

# instructions
 - grate the ginger.
 - boil the water and add the ginger and sugar
 - let it cool to room temperature
 - pour it into the fermentation vessel
 - add some of the gingerbug
 - let ferment for 3 weeks
 - bottle the gingerbeer
